What is TalkItOut about?
Talkspace makes therapy available and affordable for all. Our mission is to provide more people with convenient access to licensed therapists who can help those in need live a happier and healthier life. We are not trying to replace in-office therapy. Many people prefer that, which is fine. We created TalkItOut so more people could benefit from therapy sessions via chat, fungames, and overcome their day-to-day challenges in a stigma-free environment. With TalkItOut, you can send a therapist text messages in a private, text-based chat room. TalkItOut stands out because of the affordability and convenience it provides.
How can TalkItOut improve my life?
We have thousands of licensed therapists, many of whome are trained in multiple areas of expertise. Also we provide different sections for the user to look into such as:

  • Online games
  • Motivational quotes
  • Blog writing
Are TalkItOut therapists licensed?
Absolutely! At TalkItOut we have an extensive, nationwide network of licensed therapists. All TalkIyOut therapists have been carefully vetted and trained to use TalkItOut. We do not use interns or therapists who are not fully licensed and still under supervision. We fully ensure that our service is effective.
